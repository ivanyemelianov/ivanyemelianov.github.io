Plan For 6 Months
Month 1:

Vocabulary: Learn 2000 words (About 33 words a day since you already know about 1000-1200)

Pronunciation: Read a short story every day and read 3 sentences of a book

  1. Read 3 sentences from Harry Potter
  2. Collect new words
  3. Practice new words
  4. Read them again and see if you comfortably understand it
  5. Read a story out load once
  6. Collect new words
  7. Practice new words
  8. Read it to me to work on pronunciation

Speaking: none

Grammar: none

End of the month take the words test for A1, A2
